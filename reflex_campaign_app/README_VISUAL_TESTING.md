## Quality Metrics Explained

### Overall Score Calculation
- **Images**: 40% weight (URL generation, prompt quality, style appropriateness)
- **Videos**: 30% weight (concept detail, duration fit, description completeness)  
- **Text**: 30% weight (word count optimization, tone match, platform fit)

### Quality Thresholds
- **90-100**: Production excellence
- **80-89**: Excellent quality (current: 84/100)
- **60-79**: Good quality with minor improvements
- **40-59**: Moderate quality needing enhancement
- **0-39**: Low quality requiring significant work
