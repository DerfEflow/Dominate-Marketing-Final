Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 8, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Account Information
  • Email address and name (via Google OAuth or direct signup)
  • Profile picture (if provided through Google)
  • Business information you voluntarily provide
Marketing Profile Data (Optional)
  • Company details, industry, size, revenue range
  • Marketing budget and goals
  • Target audience descriptions
  • Contact preferences and consent choices
Usage Data
  • Campaign creation and content generation history
  • Feature usage and performance analytics
  • Login times and subscription activity

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: Generate personalized marketing content and campaigns
  • Personalization: Improve AI recommendations based on your business profile
  • Communications: Send service updates and marketing (only with your consent)
  • Analytics: Understand platform usage to improve features
  • Support: Provide customer service and technical assistance

3. Information Sharing

We share data with:
  • AI Providers: OpenAI, Google, and other AI services process your content to generate marketing materials
  • Social Platforms: When you authorize posting, we share content with Facebook, Instagram, X, TikTok, LinkedIn
  • Payment Processors: Stripe processes subscription payments (we don't store card details)
  • Analytics: Anonymized usage data for platform improvement
We never share:
  • Your personal contact information with third parties for their marketing
  • Detailed business information outside of necessary service delivery
  • Generated content with other users or competitors

4. Data Sources We Access

  • Public Business Data: Websites, social media profiles, public business listings
  • Trend Data: Public social media trends, viral content, and cultural moments
  • Competitor Analysis: Publicly available marketing materials and website content
  • Market Research: Industry reports and public marketing data

5. Data Security

  • All data transmitted using HTTPS encryption
  • OAuth tokens stored securely with industry-standard practices
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • No plain-text password storage (OAuth authentication preferred)
  • Database backups encrypted and access-controlled

6. Your Rights and Choices

Account Control
  • Update or delete your profile information anytime
  • Revoke social media authorizations through account settings
  • Cancel subscription and delete account through dashboard
Communication Preferences
  • Opt out of marketing emails and SMS at any time
  • Control newsletter and product update preferences
  • Essential service communications cannot be disabled
Data Portability
  • Export your generated content and campaign data
  • Download your marketing profile information
  • Request data deletion (subject to legal retention requirements)

7. Cookies and Tracking

  • Essential Cookies: Login sessions and security features
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve the platform
  • Preferences: Remember your settings and customizations
  • No third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels

8. International Users

  • Data processed in the United States with appropriate safeguards
  • GDPR rights respected for European users
  • Data Processing Addendum available for Enterprise customers

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for businesses and users 18+. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Third-Party Links

Our platform may link to external websites. This privacy policy does not cover third-party sites. Review their privacy policies independently.

11. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Personal data deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymized Analytics: May be retained longer for service improvement
  • Legal Requirements: Some data retained as required by law

12. Changes to Privacy Policy

We'll notify you of significant privacy policy changes via email or platform notification. Continued use indicates acceptance.

13. Contact Information

Questions about privacy? Contact us through:

  • Account dashboard support system
  • Email provided in your account settings
  • Privacy-specific inquiries handled within 5 business days

14. Compliance

We comply with applicable privacy laws including GDPR, CCPA, and other regional requirements. Enterprise customers can request additional compliance documentation.
